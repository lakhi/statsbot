Dear student,

with the use of the web service “StatsBot” you agree that your first name, surname, matriculation number, chat histories, and number of tokens used will be stored in a web database of the Vienna University Computer Center (ZID). The storage of this data is necessary to allow you access to your own chat histories from the past. To generate responses from “StatsBot”, your chat messages are sent to a large language model from Microsoft Azure and processed on their servers. For this process, the data protection regulations of the University of Vienna for Microsoft Azure Cloud Services apply (https://zid.univie.ac.at/en/azure/data-protection-and-data-security/). It should be noted that “StatsBot” does not transfer any personal data to such cloud services, and the data centers of all services are located within the EU.

In addition, with the use of the web service “StatsBot” you agree that all your chat histories until end of June 2027 can be exported and analyzed for the purpose of scientific research on how users interact with “StatsBot” and which kinds of queries they make (for further information, see https://osf.io/v8ydk/). In addition, anonymous course data (e.g. data relating to enrollment in and withdrawal from statistics tutorials) and anonymized questionnaire data are linked to chat histories in order to better analyze usage patterns. For this, your chat history will be pseudonymized, removing all personal information attached to it, and stored on a password-protected local storage medium until end of 2027. You can request the deletion of your data until end of July 2027 by contacting the coordinating team under daniel.reiter@univie.ac.at. After this, your chat histories will be anonymized and added to a dataset of anonymized students chat histories, which may be publicly shared (e.g., on OSF) in accordance with open science practices. Your pseudonymized chat histories will be permanently deleted in this process.
