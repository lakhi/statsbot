The web service “StatsBot” stores first names, surnames, matriculation numbers, chat histories, and a number of tokens used in a web database of the Vienna University Computer Center (ZID). The storage of this data is necessary to allow student to access their own chat histories from the past. In addition, chat histories are exported and analyzed for scientific purposes without personal reference (anonymized). The anonymized data will be retained for a minimum of 5 years. The anonymized dataset may be publicly shared in accordance with open science practices. Participants can request the deletion of their data within three months after their usage of StatsBot, by contacting the coordinator, who is listed in the StatsBot guidebook. The data protection regulations of the University of Vienna for Microsoft Azure Cloud Services apply to the generation of StatsBot responses. It should be noted that the StatsBot does not transfer any personal data to cloud services, the data centers of the services are located within the EU.
